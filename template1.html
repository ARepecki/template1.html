Breakfast: Salmon and Cream Cheese Bagels (high protein)

Quick and easy morning time favorites.

Prep Time: 7 mins Cook Time: 3 mins Total Time: 10 mins Serving: 2

Pickled Cucumber: 2 baby cucumbers 2 tbsp cider vinegar 1 tbsp evoo pinch salt pinch black pepper Salmon and Cream Cheese Bagels: 2 ptoeing bagel thins (sliced in half) 2.5 oz cream cheese 2 (3.5 oz) salmon filets Toppings: 1 c (packed) arugula leaves 2 tbsp hot sauce 1 tbsp mixed black and white sesame seeds salt to taste pepper to taste Instructions: 1. Chop cucumbers into bite-size pieces. Place in a small bowl. 2. Pour the cider vinegar and oil over the cucumber. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, cider vinegar, evoo, salt, and pepper. 3. Stir together and put to one side. 4. Toast bagels to your liking. Place on two plates, spread cream cheese on bagels. 5. Top with salmon and arugula. 6. Add cucumber to the plates and pour any of the vingegar/oil mixture left in the bowl over the arugula. 7. Drizzle hot sauce over the salmon and cucumber. 8. Sprinkle on sesame seeds, salt, and pepper, then serve.
